About Grass for Hay

Supporting livestock farmers with knowledge about nutritious grasses for hay.

Our Mission

The goal of this project is to educate farmers, students, and researchers about improved grass species that can be used to produce high-quality hay. By promoting sustainable forage production, we aim to improve livestock health and support food security.

Why Hay is Important

Hay plays a critical role in feeding livestock during dry seasons and times when fresh pasture is not available. Properly selected and harvested grasses provide essential nutrients for dairy and beef cattle, goats, and sheep.

Benefits of Improved Grasses

  • Higher yields of hay per acre.
  • Better nutritional content for animals.
  • Faster regrowth after cutting.
  • Adaptability to different climates and soils.
  • Improved soil fertility and erosion control.
