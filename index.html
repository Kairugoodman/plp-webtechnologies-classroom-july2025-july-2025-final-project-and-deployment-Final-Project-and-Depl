Why Grass for Hay?

Hay is a vital food resource for livestock, especially during dry seasons. Improved grasses such as Nandi Setaria and Boma Rhodes provide high yields, better nutrition, and resilience in different climates.

Featured Grass Types

Nandi Setaria

High-yield, drought-tolerant grass ideal for hay production.

Boma Rhodes

Fast-growing and highly nutritious for livestock feed.

Kikuyu Grass

Resilient, widely available grass suitable for grazing and hay.
