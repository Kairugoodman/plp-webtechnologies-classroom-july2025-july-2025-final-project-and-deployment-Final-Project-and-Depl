Our Top 5 Grasses

Nandi Setaria

A high-yield, drought-tolerant grass with excellent regrowth ability.

Boma Rhodes

Known for its fast growth and high nutritional value, ideal for hay and grazing.

Kikuyu Grass

A resilient grass that thrives in many soils, widely used for hay and pasture.

Lucerne (Alfalfa)

A protein-rich forage legume that boosts animal growth and milk production.

Desmodium

Excellent for hay and soil improvement, often intercropped with maize.
